About Us

Freaky.lb is a bold fashion brand born in Lebanon, dedicated to helping rebels and free spirits express their authentic selves through unique, edgy designs. We believe fashion should be a form of self-expression, not conformity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to break free from conventional fashion norms and embrace their unique style. We create premium streetwear that make a statement and inspire confidence.

Quality Promise

Every piece in our collection is crafted with premium materials and attention to detail. We're committed to delivering exceptional quality that stands the test of time and frequent wear.

Shipping & Returns

We offer only in lebanon shipping with tracking. No refunds unless we made a mistake in the order that you purchased and deposit is 50% of your order. Our customer service team is here to help with any questions or concerns.
